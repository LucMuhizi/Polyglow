## Research

We commission and conduct original research on Agency, Authority, and
Architecture — the structural design choices that produce or prevent
inequality. Research work is delivered as long-form reports, brief policy
papers, and peer-reviewed journal contributions.

## Training

We run workshops and seminars for policy teams, executive leadership, and
institutional change-makers. Topics include feminist governance, structural
equity assessment, designing for care, and translating frameworks into
action.

## Consultancy

We help institutions retrofit existing systems so that they become
structurally equitable. Engagements typically begin with a structural equity
audit and conclude with implementation support.

If you would like to start a project, please [contact us](/contact/).
