## Rigour

Every claim, framework, and recommendation is grounded in evidence and
reasoning. We never blur an opinion into a fact. Where the evidence is thin,
we say so plainly.

## Future orientation

We design for institutions that will need to function in a world noticeably
different from our own. Today's intervention should not lock in tomorrow's
compromise.

## Structural equity

We tune every system we touch against a single question: who benefits, who
carries, and who is left out. We name the answer before we name the policy.

## Accountability

We publish the methodology, the data, and the disagreements. Where there is
a known limit, we name the limit. Where we change our minds on a question,
we say so.
