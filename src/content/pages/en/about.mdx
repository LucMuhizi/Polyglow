Nite Tanzarn is an intellectual infrastructure for research, policy
transformation, institutional retrofit, and governance innovation. We advance
Agency, Authority, and Architecture as design levers to dismantle structural
inequality and design systems for equity.

Our work spans research, training, and consultancy on gender equality and
structural equity. We produce long-form essays, original research, policy
briefs, talks, and seminars. Every project starts with a question, and the
question is never about a single individual — it is about a system.

## Who we work with

We work with policy teams, executive leadership, and institutional
change-makers who recognise that incremental reform cannot resolve what
structural design produced. Our approach is rigorous, evidence-led, and
deliberately future-oriented.

## How we work

We begin with a structural equity audit, naming the distributions of agency,
authority, and architecture inside the institution. From there, we work with
the client to retrofit each system against a single question: who benefits,
who carries, and who is left out.
